Notes

Prefixes and suffixes:

  • can be any length
  • are joined to their inputs to indicate their connection
  • are greyed-out to indicate that they cannot be edited
  • are bordered to avoid ragged-left form field alignment
  • don't have a focus state to indicate that only the input is editable
